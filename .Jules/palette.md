## 2025-05-15 - [Copy to Clipboard Fallback]
**Learning:** Providing a "Copy to Clipboard" micro-interaction serves as a critical fallback for automated integrations (like "Push to CRM"). It empowers users when APIs fail or when working with external tools not yet integrated, significantly reducing friction.
**Action:** Include a copy-to-clipboard option for any AI-generated content. Use `aria-label` for screen readers and provide immediate visual feedback (e.g., tooltip change or icon color change) to confirm the action was successful.
